About Me

Hello! My name is Tyler Valley. I am a software developer that specializes in progressive web applications. I have 5 years of experience in the MEAN stack (MySql, Express, Angular, and Node), as well as experience in Web3, React, and other JavaScript frameworks.
I am originally from and live in Alabama, and have worked remotely from here for the past 2 years. I also do some freelance work when I can, mostly building custom websites and apps.
I love solving problems, and I approach all of my projects as a problem to be solved. I thoroughly enjoy working with people to create solutions to problems, and to create great products that many people can enjoy.
I approach everything I do with my work as a learning experience, and I strive to improve at what I do constantly. I'm always learning from other people, and from solving the problems I encounter when developing applications. While my focus is on the front end side of web applications development, I also have the ability to work as a full stack or back end developer.
I look forward to working on new and exciting projects, I'm always learning, and I'm always having fun with programming.
